Who We Are

Our Story

In the summer of 2017, 15-year-old activist Jamie Margolin decided it was time for leaders to take the climate crisis seriously and for youth to take charge of their future.

She came up with a vision for a youth-led movement targeted at bringing youth face-to-face with decision makers and the forefront of the conversation on climate action. Word of Jamie’s idea spread to other climate-conscious youth who rallied around the cause and formed a team, determined to start a movement.

By the end of the summer, their team was growing across the country from coast to coast. Now, there is a fully fledged team determined to lift the voices of youth:

At the start of 2018, a few members traveled to Washington, D.C. to lay key groundwork for this growing movement. They made more connections, learned about major players in environmental advocacy and met each other in person for the very first time. The Zero Hour team has become more than just an organization—we’ve become a family.

We want our leaders to make the right decisions in regards to our future—we aim to give them solutions and be a force that cannot be ignored. We’ve been growing more and working to make this a reality, but our story is not complete. You can be a part of our movement too.

This vision depends on people like you willing to support and uplift youth by making this vision your own. Join us and let’s write the next chapter of our story together.
