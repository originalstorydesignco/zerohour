Who We Are

Our Story

In the summer of 2017, 15-year-old activist Jamie Margolin decided it was time for leaders to take the climate crisis seriously and for youth to take charge of their future.

#ThisisZeroHour is a youth-led movement, targeted at bringing youth face to face with decision makers and to the forefront of the conversation on climate change. We want our leaders to make the right decisions in regards to what affects our future - we aim to give them solutions and be a force that cannot be ignored.

Word of Jamie’s vision spread to other climate-woke youth who rallied around the idea and formed a team, determined to start a movement.

By the end of Summer 2017, their team was growing across the country from coast to coast.

Now there was a full fledged team determined to lift the voices of youth:

Jamie / Founder

Nadia / Branding & Partnerships

Madelaine / Fundraising

Zanagee / Logistics

In late January 2018, a few members traveled to Washington, D.C. to lay a groundwork for this growing movement. They made more connections, learned about key players in environmental advocacy and met each other in person for the very first time. The Zero Hour team had become more than just an organization - they became a family.

We’ve been growing more and working more ever since- but our story is not complete. You can be a part of our movement too.

The movement depends on people like you willing to support and uplift youth by making this vision your own. Join us and let’s write the next chapter of our story together.
