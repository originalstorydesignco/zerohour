Sister Chapters

Chapters

Zero Hour sister chapters are an opportunity to be a leader in your community and take a stand for our generation and all those affected by climate change. Starting or joining a Zero Hour sister chapter allows one to become educated and take part in an inclusive climate justice movement. This will increase your knowledge of this issue, utilize your skills to organize events and actions, and aid your capability of voicing your opinions as a youth involved in their future.

You can apply to be a Zero Hour sister chapter here!

Once you fill out the Google form, the Global Outreach team will look over your application and decide if we can add your location to the Zero Hour International Network.

If your application is accepted as a Sister Chapter, the team will add you to our interactive sister action map so people can find and contact you if they want to get involved in your area, share your specific location on all the documents with the other sister chapters and ask to get on a call to discuss your specific ideas and local actions.
