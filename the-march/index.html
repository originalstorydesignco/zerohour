The March

July 21. Washington D.c.

The major purpose of this collective is to hit the streets in Washington, D.C. We want you to join us on July 21 to show the power of youth mobilization. Save. The. Date.

In Washington, D.C., we will march on the National Mall and speak to our elected representatives directly on Capital Hill in a lobby day the day before the march. At the same time, youth will hold marches, rallies, and other youth-led actions in cities all across the world. This page will be updated with details as they get figured out. In the meantime, contact us at info@thisiszerohour.org to learn about organizing an action in your city.
