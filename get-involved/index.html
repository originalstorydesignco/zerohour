Donate

One of the best ways that you can support climate action is by offering financial resources to those who don’t have them. We’re young. We’re either making minimum wage or focusing on schoolwork. So essentially the only way that youth-led solutions are possible is through the financial backing of adults. Your support will ensure that youth activists can participate in the day of action in Washington, D.C., and provide a foundation for this growing movement.

Please make a donation to help us confront our leaders and amplify the youth voice in the climate movement. Every donation counts and no amount is too small. Hit us with a nickel. We’ll put it to good use.
