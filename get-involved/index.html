Support our work

Zero Hour is a movement led by youth who are determined to advocate for concrete solutions to stop climate change and hold our adults and elected representatives accountable for their inaction surrounding it.

We have a vision and it starts with mobilizing youth around the country to take action this summer for the Zero Hour March and day of action. Your support will ensure that youth activists can participate in Washington, D.C., and in cities around the country to make sure that youth voices are heard loud and clear!

Can’t make a monetary donation? We would also love to accept in-kind donations of resources you or your organization may be able to give. For instance—houses and living spaces in Washington, D.C. to host young activists for our day of action, cars and other means of transportation, resources and supplies for training and workshops we host. We promise we’ll put every dollar to good use! Please reach out to us at info@thisiszerohour.org if you want to donate any of the above items or other resources.
