Enough is enough. We cannot afford to wait any longer for leaders to protect the natural resources we need to live. We demand immediate action at all levels of government, business, and society to secure these for current and future generations.

YMCAN is a call for emergency action. Young people are already suffering the consequences of the climate crisis in the form of deadly extreme weather events, threats to food security, public health threats, contamination of drinking water and much more. Day by day, these hazards are getting worse and threatening our entire generation and our future.

The impact of the climate crisis is profoundly unequal. YMCAN recognizes the complex intersections with power structures of gender, race, class, and systems of exclusion and inequality. We seek solutions that address these inequalities and ensure the basic environmental needs of all people.

The communities on the front lines of the climate crisis are actively working to create solutions. YMCAN centers the unique wisdom, experience, and leadership of these communities in our efforts to make impactful change.

The Youth Movement for Climate Action Now is a message, directly from youth to adult leaders: we will hold you accountable for your legacy of destruction. We call on you to protect our right to a livable future.
